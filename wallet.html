Making Paying Easy

Digital Wallet is a convenient way to pay with your CIBM Bank debit card(s). If your phone is in your hand or your smart watch is on your wrist, so are your activated cards. It is safe and simple, allowing you to carry less and get more. Link your personal, business, and HSA cards today.

Activate your card(s) today with:

  • Apple Pay™
  • Google Pay™
  • Samsung Pay™
