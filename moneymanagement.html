Money Management

Cash Management

Online banking platform includes:

  • Stop payment processing, bill pay and alerts
  • Automated Clearing House
  • Account transfers and reconciliation
  • Wire transfers
  • Positive pay services
  • Remote deposit
  • Electronic statements

Sweeps

  • Setup automatic transfer from one account to another to maintain a target balance
  • Create ability to maintain a zero balance account with automatic transfers
  • Minimize interest expense or increase cash flow with automatic sweeps to and from your line of credit

Capital Markets

  • Repurchase agreement options
  • US Treasury Bill and government agency discount notes - one year and less

These are not a bank deposit, obligation or guaranteed by the bank and are NOT FDIC insured. These investment products are subject to investment risk, including potential principal loss.
