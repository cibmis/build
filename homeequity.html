Home Equity

Get something back for all you have out into your home.

Home Equity Loan

Use the equity in your home as collateral to guarantee the loan.

  • Fixed rate and fixed term
  • Consistent payment for easy budgeting
  • Borrow one lump sum
  • Great for a single purpose, large home improvement, or consolidation of debt

Home equity line of credit

Use a little or use a lot; this loan type allows you to borrow only what you need right now.

  • Revolving line of credit
  • Fixed length varible rate
  • Payments are interest only
  • Access your funds via transfer to your CIBM Bank account
