Commercial Real Estate Financing

Each business is different as are their real estate needs. No matter how complex your needs are, CIBM has the expertise and resources to provide your business with a tailor made solution. Our lenders have specific knowledge and expertise in working with a wide range or property types, including:

  • Construction financing
  • Owner occupied office buildings
  • Plants, industrial, and manufacturing facilities
  • Apartments buildings and multi-family housing
  • Flex space
  • Retail
  • Community Development projects

A CIBM commercial real estate loan provides funds to construct, acquire, or refinance your business property. Loan terms and payment amortizations are matched to the needs of your business and the characteristics for your property. CIBM offers competitive floating and fixed rate interest options. CIBM also has the tools to allow you to hedge your interest rate risk, including the ability to secure longer term fixed rates.

All term are subject to CIBM Bank approval. Specific collateral and other requirements may apply.

Whether you are acquiring, remodeling, or refinancing a property, CIBM has variety of financing options to match your need. To learn more, contact a CIBM bank representative today.
