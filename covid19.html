Branch Locations and Internal Efforts

Our branch locations remain accessible. In an effort to promote social distancing and prioritize the health and well-being of our communities, some of our office lobbies will be closed to the public. Those offices will operate under drive-through window access only. In these cases, all banking activity (with the exception of accessing safe deposit boxes) can be serviced via the drive-through window. An updated list of those facilities is available here.

  • Effective 3/17/20 – Wheaton – 330 S. Naperville Rd. Suite 100 Wheaton, IL 60187
  • Effective 3/18/20 – Midtown – 302 W. Springfield Ave. Champaign, IL 61820
  • Effective 3/19/20 - Indianapolis - 5120 E. 82nd Street Indianapolis, IN 46250
  • Effective 3/19/20 - Peoria - 7815 N. Knoxville Peoria, IL 61614

When visiting a drive-through only facility, for faster service and smoother operation, please utilize Lane 1 to conduct personal banking activity and non-customer check cashing. Please conduct teller transactions in all other lanes.

If you are visiting a branch location, please know that in addition to regular cleaning procedures, increased sanitation measures are being added to all locations and our staff regularly wipe down common areas. If you do not wish to enter the branch, we encourage you to utilize our drive-through windows where available.

All staff members have been advised to remain at home if they are not feeling well.

Protect yourself from scams

Please know that we won’t ask for confidential information -- such as your name, password, PIN or other account information -- if we reach out to you.

CIBM Bank has very detailed contingency plans in place for situations such as this, and we are here to support and assist you to ensure you do not experience a disruption in service. We will continue to monitor this quickly evolving situation, and take guidance from public health authorities.
